SMART Grid System

Smart grid is the intelligence of power grid, also known as "power grid 2.0". It is built on the basis of integrated, high-speed two-way communication network. Through the application of advanced sensing and measurement technology, advanced equipment technology, advanced control method and advanced decision support system technology, it can realize the reliability, safety, economy, high efficiency and reliability of power grid. The goal of environment-friendly and safe use, its main features include self-healing, encouraging and including users, resisting attacks, providing power quality to meet the needs of users in the 21st century, allowing access to various forms of power generation, starting the power market and optimizing and efficient operation of assets.
